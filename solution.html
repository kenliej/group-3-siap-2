Our Simple Digital Solution

How Our System Works

We use Google Sheets or Microsoft Excel to record sales and stock updates instantly. This simple yet powerful approach allows us to maintain accurate inventory records, track sales patterns, and make informed business decisions all without expensive software or complicated training.

How It Works

Three simple steps to streamline your inventory and sales

Step 1: Record Every Sale

Log each transaction digitally as it happens. Enter product name, quantity, price, and customer details into your spreadsheet.

Step 2: Automatic Stock Alerts

The system automatically deducts stock and highlights low inventory items with color alerts, so you never miss a restock opportunity.

Step 3: Weekly Summaries

Generate comprehensive weekly sales reports to analyze trends, identify top products, and plan your inventory accordingly.
